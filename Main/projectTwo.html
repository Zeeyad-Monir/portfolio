About PackIT

PackIT is a scalable app framework designed for educational institutions with dining services to address food waste and food insecurity. By redistributing surplus food as affordable meals and fostering campus-wide engagement through structured volunteer opportunities, PackIT aligns with the UN Sustainable Development Goal 2 (End Hunger, Achieve Food Security, and Promote Sustainable Agriculture).

Core Features:

  • Food Redistribution: Students can browse available meals from surplus food categorized by meal type (breakfast, lunch, dinner), complete with real-time inventory updates, allergen details, and portion sizes.
  • Student & Staff Dual-View System: Students reserve meals and sign up for volunteering while staff oversee food inventory, manage volunteers, and track meal distribution.
  • Volunteer Engagement & Rewards: Students can earn Co-Curricular Record (CCR) credits and take part in a points-based gamification system to encourage consistent volunteering.
  • Accessibility & Error Prevention: Text-to-speech, adjustable font sizes, high-contrast modes, and real-time validation reduce errors and accommodate diverse user needs.
  • User-Friendly Navigation & Notifications: A consistent “Return to Home” button and push notifications remind users of meal availability and upcoming shifts.
  • Institutional & Community Impact: Unlike general food-waste apps, PackIT is specifically tailored for universities to reduce waste, improve sustainability, and involve the entire campus community.
