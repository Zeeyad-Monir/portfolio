Project Overview

JADE DECO is a jade-based home decor/incense brand rooted in craftsmanship, cultural heritage, and a modern aesthetic. The business goal was to expand into the North American market and grow awareness in a space where new brands must earn trust fast - especially when the product's value is tied to meaning, material, and story.

How Might We

How might we help JADE DECO enter the North American market using a compelling storytelling campaign that creates meaningful content, connects with users, and increases brand awareness?

Success was defined around three outcomes:

01

Build trust for a new-to-market brand

Credibility, clarity, and authenticity signals needed to be visible early.

02

Increase visibility through familiar discovery channels

Social platforms were the clearest place to establish awareness and repeat exposure.

03

Create a cohesive story system that can scale across touchpoints

The campaign needed to stay consistent from social to microsite to shop.

Design Strategy & Iteration
01

Brand Diagnosis (What We Learned Early)

JADE DECO's core advantage is cultural meaning + craftsmanship, but entering a new market introduces friction: users need reassurance, context, and a clear reason to believe. Early analysis pointed to gaps in trust-building content, story depth, and localized relevance for Canadian/North American audiences.

02

Audience Insights

We shaped two core customer mindsets to guide messaging, content tone, and trust signals:

Persona 1: The Amateur Decorator

A style-driven shopper who wants decor that feels personal and "made for their space," but needs better product clarity online to feel confident buying.

Persona 2: The Cautious Cultural Shopper

A values-driven shopper who cares deeply about authenticity and cultural meaning, but is skeptical when brands lack clear proof, truthful description, and heritage storytelling.

03

Customer Journey (Where People Hesitate)

We mapped the gift-buyer journey and found that uncertainty spikes during evaluation: "Is this brand legit?", "Will it match the photos?", "What if it arrives different?" The biggest opportunities were trust signals, clear storytelling, and social proof that reduce perceived risk.

Key friction points

  • Low brand familiarity = hesitation
  • Unclear product details = reduced trust
  • "Will it look like the photos?" anxiety
  • Gift purchase = higher fear of regret
04

Strategy: Why a Storytelling Campaign

A storytelling campaign was chosen because it's uniquely effective for brands where meaning is the product. Storytelling helps JADE DECO translate heritage and craftsmanship into a memorable identity, build emotional connection, and extend across multiple touchpoints without losing consistency.

05

3 Potential Solutions

Concept 1 - Connecting Through Social Media

A discovery-first approach that builds brand presence on platforms customers already use. The concept focuses on consistent visuals, short-form story content, and shoppable pathways to move users from curiosity -> interest -> site visit.

A social-first strategy to increase reach and familiarity through consistent storytelling and visually cohesive content.

Concept 2 - Interactive Brand Storytelling Page

A credibility-first approach focused on explaining "why JADE DECO" through an interactive narrative experience. This direction emphasizes sourcing, heritage, craftsmanship, and product meaning to reduce skepticism and build trust.

An "Our Story" experience designed to make cultural meaning and craftsmanship legible - turning uncertainty into confidence.

Concept 3 - QR Code Community Board

A retention/engagement approach that extends the experience beyond checkout. A QR touchpoint connects customers to a community board where they can view styled spaces, share inspiration, and see real customer context around the product.

A post-purchase community concept that encourages participation, inspiration, and brand affinity through real customer styling.

The Solution

What Changed During Iteration

As we evaluated the concepts, a clear pattern emerged: the strongest growth lever wasn't one single channel - it was a cohesive story system anchored by a central narrative and deployed across the platforms customers already trust.

01

Brand Film

Carries the emotional story and gives the campaign a central narrative anchor.

02

Instagram

Drives discovery, repeat exposure, and a familiar content rhythm for new audiences.

03

Microsite Hub

Concentrates credibility, storytelling depth, and clearer next actions in one place.

04

QR Touchpoint

Acts as an optional bridge for offline events and pop-ups, extending the story into physical contexts.
