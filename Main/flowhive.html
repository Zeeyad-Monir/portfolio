Flow Hive Companion

Role: UX/UI Designer
Tools: Figma, Miro, Adobe Creative Suite
Date: Sept 2024 - Dec 2024
Figma Slide Deck

Overview

Flow Hive Companion is a two-part experience designed for new urban beekeepers, helping them assess if they are ready for a hive and then maintain healthy forage around it. The project focuses on reducing early hive failure by guiding users through suitability checks, forage education, and ongoing space management with the help of a friendly mascot, Buzzy.

Design Process

This was a Figma-led prototype shaped through a human-centered design lens, using Miro for mapping flows and Adobe Creative Suite for visual assets.

  • User Research: Defined a primary persona (Anthony) and mapped pain points across pre-purchase anxiety, regulations, and forage uncertainty.
  • Journey Mapping: Built end-to-end flows for both the web-based readiness check and the mobile app ecosystem.
  • Iterative Prototyping: Evolved from low-fidelity flows to interactive high-fidelity screens, validating clarity and usefulness through feedback cycles.

The Solution

  • Pre-purchase Guidance: A Flow-branded web experience that walks users through location, regulations, space, and forage readiness, giving personalized "fit" feedback and educating them with Buzzy's forage scenarios.
  • Post-purchase Ecosystem: A mobile app that lets users simulate their hive space, scan and discover bee-friendly plants, track tasks tied to Buzzy's health, and explore a map of nearby Flow users and their forage coverage.
