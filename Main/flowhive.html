Overview

Flow Hive Companion is a two-part experience designed for new urban beekeepers, helping them assess if they are ready for a hive and then maintain healthy forage around it. The project focuses on reducing early hive failure by guiding users through suitability checks, forage education, and ongoing space management with the help of a friendly mascot, Buzzy.

Design Process
01

User Research

Defined a primary persona (Anthony) and mapped pain points across pre-purchase anxiety, local regulations, and forage uncertainty that new beekeepers face.

02

Journey Mapping

Built end-to-end flows for both the web-based readiness check and the mobile app ecosystem, ensuring a seamless transition from consideration to active beekeeping.

03

Design & Prototyping

Created a Figma-led prototype shaped through a human-centered design lens, using Miro for mapping flows and Adobe Creative Suite for visual assets and Buzzy illustrations.

04

Iterative Validation

Evolved from low-fidelity flows to interactive high-fidelity screens, validating clarity and usefulness through feedback cycles with potential users.

Reasoning

Built for resilient hives. Each feature reflects evidence about forage availability, seasonal nutrition, and the impact of active stewardship on colony survival.

The Solution

Buzzy Health Dashboard

A personalized dashboard that highlights immediate tasks tied to Buzzy's health, helping keepers stay on top of hive care with clear visual cues and actionable reminders.

AR Plant Scanner

Point your camera at any plant to get instant identification and bee-safety guidance. The scanner overlays detection results in real space, making forage discovery intuitive.

Community Forage Map

Discover nearby Flow keepers and coordinate forage coverage. The heatmap surfaces gaps around your hive so the community can fill them together for healthier bees.
